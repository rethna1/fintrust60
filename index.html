Quick Summary

How this ULIP model works

  • Annual premium is invested each year (you can add step-ups).
  • Fund growth is simulated using the expected annual return, reduced by the fund charge percentage.
  • Estimated charges show cumulative fund charges over the term.
  • Recommended death benefit uses a simple rule: higher of fund value or 105% of premiums paid (common illustrative rule).

Note: This is a simplified model for planning. Actual ULIP calculations include allocation, policy administration charges, mortality charges, switching costs, partial withdrawals and other rules that vary by insurer.
